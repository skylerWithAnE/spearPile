Fourscore and seven years ago.

All The Goths, three nine.
Shylock, four zero.

Act I: four one.

Scene I: four two.

[Enter All The Goths and Shylock]

All The Goths: You are a fancy peach.
Shylock: Let us proceed to act I, scene II.

[Exeunt]

Scene II: four three.
[Enter All The Goths]

All The Goths: I am a king!


[Exit All The Goths]

Act II: four four.

Scene I: four five.

[Enter Shylock]

Shylock: We shall proceed to act I, scene II.

[Exit Shylock]
