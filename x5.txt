Fourscore and seven years ago.

All The Goths, five zero.
Shylock, five one.
Soothsayer, five two.
Gertrude, five three.

Act I: five four.

Scene I: five five.

[Enter All The Goths and Shylock]
All The Goths: Am I as fancy as a toad?
Shylock: Is All The Goths better than a toad?
All The Goths: Are you better than a fancy purse?
Shylock: Is All The Goths more furry than you?
All The Goths: Is a fancy lad worse than All The Goths?
[Exeunt]
