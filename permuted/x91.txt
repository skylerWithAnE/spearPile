Fourscore and seven years ago.

All The Goths, The counter.
Shylock, accumulates the value.
Claudius, another value.

Act I: The Play Opens.

Scene I: We Begin. Yes, we do.

[Enter All The Goths and Shylock]

All The Goths: 
    I am a fancy fancy king.
    Am I at most as good as a fancy  king?
    If so, you are a queen.

[Exit Shylock]
[Enter Claudius]

All The Goths: If not, you are a fair queen.

[Exeunt]
