Fourscore and seven years ago.

All The Goths, a variable which eventually is sixty five.
Shylock, a new line.
Claudius, a temporary variable.

Act I: The Play Opens.

Scene I: We Begin.

[Enter All The Goths]

All The Goths: All The Goths, you are a fancy fancy fancy fancy fancy fancy king. You are
    the sum of yourself and a king. Speak your mind.
    
[Enter Shylock and Claudius]

All The Goths: Shylock, You are a fancy fancy fancy queen. You are as good as the sum of 
    yourself and a fancy queen. Speak your mind. 
    
All The Goths: Claudius, open your mind. You are as good as the
    sum of yourself and a queen. Speak your mind.
    
All The Goths: Shylock, speak your mind. Are you as good as a lad!

All The Goths: Claudius, Listen to your heart. 
    You are as good as the sum of yourself and a king.
    Open your heart.

All The Goths: Shylock, Speak your mind.


    
[Exeunt]
