Fourscore and seven years ago.

All The Goths, a variable.
Shylock, another variable.
Soothsayer, the adversary.

Act I: The first act.
Scene I: The first scene.

[Enter All The Goths and Shylock]

All The Goths: I am a fancy mighty king. You are a fancy queen.
Shylock: Am I as good as a toad?
All The Goths: If so, remember me. Open your heart.
Shylock: Open your heart.

[Exeunt omnes]
