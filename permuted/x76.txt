Fourscore and seven years ago.

Shylock, her first instance.
All The Goths, a variable.

Act I: The first act.
Scene I: The first scene.

[Enter All The Goths and Shylock]

All The Goths: You are a toad. Are you as good as a toad? If so,
    let us proceed to Act I, Scene III.

[Exeunt]

Scene II: The second scene.

[Enter All The Goths]

All The Goths: I am a toad.

[Exit All The Goths]
