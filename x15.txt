Fourscore and seven years ago.

All The Goths, one six.
Shylock, one seven.
Soothsayer, one eight.
Gertrude, one nine.

Act I: two zero.

Scene I: two one.

[Enter All The Goths and Shylock]
All The Goths: Are you less vile than the product of a fancy lad and a vile peach?
Shylock: Are you at least as vile as the product of Soothsayer and a toad?
All The Goths: Are you at most as fancy as a fancy lad?
Shylock: Are you different from the product of Soothsayer and a toad?
[Exeunt]
