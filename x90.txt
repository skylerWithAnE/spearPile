Fourscore and seven years ago.

All The Goths, a variable in love with Shylock.
Shylock, who finds her voice.

Act I: The Play Opens.

Scene I: We Begin.

[Enter All The Goths and Shylock]

All The Goths: I am a furry cunning mountaineer. You are a lovely peach.

Shylock: You are as good as the sum of yourself and myself.

[Exeunt]
